About Me

Hi! I'm Allison O'Hanlon, a software developer with a marketing background. I'm originally from Oregon, but currently call myself a Texan. I graduated from Trinity University with a marketing and communication degree and completed the University of Texas Coding Bootcamp.

I'm the kind of person that goes to China without knowing a lick of Chinese, but can be overheard weeks later bartering along with the locals. I thrive on taking chances and challenging myself outside my comfort zone. I am at my best when I don't just take a baby step, but when I jump right in.

I love putting my hands to the keyboard - whether I'm coding, writing a blog or designing on Photoshop. I'm a doer. I have a never-ending thirst for learning, a natural tendancy of creative thinking, and a love for saying "I could make that" and making ideas realities. My favorite type of work is when I get to combine my marketing knowledge and technical skills into making something awesome.
